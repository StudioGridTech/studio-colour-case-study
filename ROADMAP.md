# Roadmap

What's shipped today, and the direction ahead. **Directional — not a commitment.**

## ✅ Available now

- Pro eyedropper with magnifier loupe + multi-pick
- WCAG / APCA contrast grader with one-click Fix
- Gradient studio — linear & radial with positioned stops
- Full colour-space support (HEX → OKLCH → Display-P3)
- Design-token export to code, across multiple builders and formats
- Saved colours, groups, and gradients

## 🔜 On the way

- More browsers
- More export targets and builder integrations
- Refinements driven by what users ask for

## 🔭 The bigger picture

Studio Colour is one piece of a larger vision for connected design tooling at **[Studio Grid](https://studiogrid.io)**. There's plenty more coming — follow along.
